Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Description

One of the easiest, cheapest things to make and delicious; this recipe will ensure you whip up perfect golden flapjacks.

Below are the ingredients, most of which you should already have.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. First add all the dry ingredients, sifting them together
  2. Create a well and add in wet ingredients, stirring them together
  3. Scoop batter onto your hot griddle
  4. After cooking for 2-3 minutes, flip
  5. When golden brown on both sides, you're all done
